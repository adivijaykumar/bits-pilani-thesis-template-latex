\begin{abstract}
The Project is an attempt to understand the physics of partilces and their interactions through the systemic study of the fields involved. It comprises of a study of the Klein-Gordon Field, the Dirac Field, and the gauge theories of Quantum Electrodynamics and Quantum Chromodynamics. 
\end{abstract}
